Who we are

We are students, cyber-sec enthusiasts, devs and programmers who believe in opensource and free distribution of knowledge and resources of the art of Hacking software, hardware, systems or companies.

Our Goal

Our goal is to accumulate and distribute resources, references, guides, techniques and opensource software/scripts/exploits and build an army of cyber ninjas.

The guides, being friendly to beginners, will also be a good reference for proffesionals in the field

You should join us

All our guides and resources will be available to contribute on GitHub, and our community is currently active on Discord. As this is a community based project, we would love your participation to help us grow.
